FAQ

Can I return my shirt?

Unfortunately, your shirt is made-to-order and all sales are final. We recommend you follow our fit guide and use a professional tailor to fill out our measurement form to ensure the proper fit.

Can I machine wash my shirt?

Yes, the materials are designed to be machined washed in cold. Hang dry, do not use the drying machine.

Do you only offer shirts in white or blue?

Yes, currently we only offer blue or white shirts to streamline our production process and keep prices low. Sign-up for our newsletter and look out for additional products in the future.
